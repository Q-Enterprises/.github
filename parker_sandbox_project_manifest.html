Core Design Pillars

The foundational logic that separates Parker's Sandbox from traditional game engines.

Deterministic World State

Every frame of simulation is derived from initial conditions and player inputs with 100% parity. This ensures that "Golden Replays" are not just recordings, but executable mathematical truths.

Inspectable Causality

Why did that block fall? Why did the agent move? Our ledger architecture logs the "Why" of every physics calculation, allowing users and agents to scrub through the cause-and-effect timeline.

AI as Witness, Not Wizard

The Agent & Analysis Layer acts as the bridge between raw data and human understanding. It exists outside the simulation loop to ensure it can observe without interference, acting as a sovereign observer of the world's causality.

Agentic Hub & Spoke Deployment

The transition to an Agentic Hub & Spoke model introduces a control plane that keeps agent insight auditable and bounded. The hub governs policy, provenance, and temporal ordering, while spokes handle domain-specific observation and tutoring without altering state.

Hub Control

Policy enforcement, ledger alignment, and deterministic sequencing.

Spoke Insight

Localized analysis, tutoring overlays, and telemetry summarization.

Metrology HUD

Project portfolio tracker for git-based atomic intelligence and temporal alignment.

Causal Attribution

Performs pattern recognition across runs to determine "why" events occur based on the Ledger.

Expert Tutoring

Translates raw telemetry into natural language explanations of complex physics interactions.

Operational Boundaries

No State Mutation

Agents cannot directly modify the world, physics rules, or player intent.

Total Transparency

Agents are forbidden from concealing state transitions, ensuring auditable truth.
